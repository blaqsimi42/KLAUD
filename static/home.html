FAQ'S

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
1. What types of products do you offer?
Ans: We offer a wide range of products, including clothing, accessories, home decor, and more. Each product is modeled to give you a clear view of how it looks when worn or used.
2. How can I purchase a product from your site?
Ans: To purchase a product, simply browse our catalog, select the item you’re interested in, and click the “Add to Cart” button. Follow the checkout process to complete your purchase.
3. What payment methods do you accept?
Ans: We accept various payment methods, including major credit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment gateways. Please check our payment options during checkout.
4. How can I track my order?
Ans: Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this tracking number to monitor your order’s status on our shipping partner's website.
5. Are the products shown in the photos the same as the ones I’ll receive?
Ans: We strive to accurately represent our products through high-quality images and detailed descriptions. However, slight variations in color or texture may occur due to lighting and screen settings.
6. What should I do if I received a damaged or incorrect item?
Ans: If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, please contact our customer support team immediately. Provide your order number and a description of the issue so we can resolve it promptly.
7. How can I leave a review for a product?
Ans: After receiving your order, you can leave a review on the product page. We appreciate your feedback and encourage you to share your experience with other customers.
